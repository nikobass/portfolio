Nicolas Vivat

Front-end Developer | Paris

After several years as an electronic technician, I chose to change my field to broaden my horizons and live from my passion. I decided to make a professional reconversion as a Web Developer. 5 months of intensive training at O'clock, recognized by the "Grande école du Numérique".

Following this, I was able to take a position as a full-time Frontend Web Developer. This allowed me to apply my knowledge and continue my evolution by discovering the Angular framework.

I then discovered a particular appetite for the front-end. After almost a year in a company, I decided to continue my training for 6 months by joining the O'clock school again. This time, focusing on javascript and react. This was for me the logical continuation in my quest for knowledge and at the same time to add skills to my junior profile.
